Sylhet Flood: Over 37,000 people still living in shelters

More than 37,000 people remained in shelter centres in flood-hit Sylhet after devastating floods swept through several parts of Bangladesh, leaving 95 people dead. According to the Sylhet District Administration, there are now 439 shelters in Sylhet district where 37,176 people are still staying in these shelters. Almost 3 million people have been rendered homeless and as many as 40,091 houses destroyed in the recent floods in Sylhet, sources said on Thursday.
